Hi, I'm Nigel.

I'm smiling because I love what I do. Every day, I get to create experiences that increase access to information and technology. Sometimes the work I do, in its own small way, helps to create a more just and inclusive world. How good is that?

I started my career in digital 9 years ago as a Content Coordinator at The Movemeber Foundation. There, I was exposed to an incredible group of people doing amazing things for digital, and for the world. I’ve taken that passion for advancing my craft while doing good with me throughout my entire career, from content into UX, design strategy, leadership and beyond.

I’m not currently looking for new full-time work opportunities, but I love to take on small freelance projects in UX, content, design or development. So if you or your small business have a problem that needs solving, or if you need a new website like this one, reach out! Let's have a chat.

Skills that pay my bills

Workshop facilitation

I've facilitated workshops with senior leaders at some of Australia's largest and most well-known companies. No problem is too big or too small that we can't work it out in a room together with a tub full of post-it notes, thinking games and catered sandwiches.

Design research and testing

Uncovering the right problems to solve is a critical skill, and mastering the tools and techniques available to uncover those problems is something I've spent years on. From qualitative ethnographic fieldwork, usability testing and interviews — to quantitative analytics, a/b testing and surveys — I can help to achieve clarity and find light in the dark.

Personas, user journeys and flows

Creating empathy for your audience and carefully shaping their foundational experience is a critical step in the design process. By taking the deep understanding gained from research and creating these powerful artefacts, I can help illuminate the unknowns about your audience and shape their ideal experience.

Wireframes, prototypes and UI design

Creating interfaces that your users will not only comprehend but love is my core specialty. There are a near endless range of patterns and possibilities, and I get a kick out of exploring as many as I can to land on a solution that meets your users needs and gives them an experience they'll want to come back for.

UX writing and copywriting

Originally coming from a content strategy and copywriting background means I take a truly content-first approach to all of my work. The interface means very little if it delivers nothing of value to the user. So whether I'm writing button copy or biographies, no job is too big or too small.

Content strategy

I can help you understand and document the structure, substance and style your content should follow in order to achieve success for your organisation. I've even consulted to advise on the structure and processes of entire content teams at large organisations, so they can create their own internal content powerhouses.

Information architecture

Making sense of mess to create simple and intuitive journeys through the content and functionality of your app or website is where I really get to geek out. There's nothing cooler than a thousand row spreadsheet to lay the foundations of your future product.

Visual design

I'm able to take a concept from words to sketches to wireframes and high-fidelity UI concepts, while working within (or extending) your existing brand style with confidence.

Front-end development

I built this website, why not yours?! After dabbling in and out of HTML and CSS for years, I finally invested in Front-End Development courses and I'm having a blast. Now I can write, design AND code simple websites just like this one!
